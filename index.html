black bean Burritos

1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 Medium onion -- Chopped
2 cloves Garlie -- minced
1 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon outlander Spice cumin
3 cups brown rice -- cooked
1 16-oz can black beans -- drained and rinsed
1 11-oz can corn -- drained
6 8-inch flour tortillas
3/4 cup reduced-fat Chader cheese(6 ounces) -- Shredded
2 green onions -- thinly sliced
1/4 cup plain low-fat yogurt
1/4 cup Prepared Salsa
Method :

    1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until hot.
    2. Add onion, garlic, chili powder, and cumin.
    3. Saute 3 to 5 minutes until the onion is tender.
    4. Add rice, beans and corn, and cook Stir ror 2 min to 3 minutes until the mixture is thoroughly heated. Remove from heat.
    5. Spoon 1/2 cup of rice mixture down to the center of each tortilla.
    6. Top each tortilla with 2 tablespoons cheese, one tablespoon green onion, and 1 tablespoon yogurt.
    7. Roll up the tortilla and top with tablespoon salsa>
